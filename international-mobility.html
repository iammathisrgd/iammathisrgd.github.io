As part of my studies at ENSEEIHT, I have to move to a foreign country during one semester to validate my international mobility.

During the summer of 2024, I did a 7-week internship in Japan, including 3 weeks of language internship and 4 weeks of internship in the hotel industry. My role was to handle the maintenance and cleaning of hotel rooms. I had to demonstrate diligence and thoroughness in my work to ensure a positive experience for the guests. I learned to work as part of a team and to be attentive in order to understand instructions despite the language barrier.

I am now thinking about my next destination. I would be interested in an internship in Northern European countries or in Asia, particularly in Japan as I loved this country.
